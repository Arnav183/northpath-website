Careers

Northpath Consulting hires people who can tell when a model is wrong. The work is remote and project-based: reviewing model outputs, writing rubrics, producing feedback data, and building the tooling around them.

Client projects come from AI labs, startups, and data vendors. Your output feeds the training and evaluation of frontier models. Most of it is covered by NDA. You see the details after signing, not before.

Who we are looking for

Evaluation and data

AI evaluation specialist

full-time or contract · remote

Evaluation specialists review model outputs against rubrics, document failure modes, and write the justifications that make a judgment usable as training data. Expect deep work in domains you know well: mathematics, code, science, finance, or writing. Strong candidates verify before they agree; they recompute the math, run the code, and check the sources.

contract · remote

Contractors produce preference rankings, rubric-scored ratings, and written critiques on active client projects. Volume matters, but accuracy matters more; every submission is reviewed before it ships. This role fits people who want flexible project work rather than a fixed schedule.

internship · remote

Interns learn eval design by doing it: drafting rubrics, testing prompts against models, and assisting with quality review under a senior consultant. The internship is project-based and sized to fit alongside coursework. No evaluation experience is required; the trial task is what we judge.

process

How applying works

  1. Submit the application form. Include links to real work: a portfolio, GitHub, papers, or writing samples. A short note beats a long cover letter.
  2. Shortlisted candidates complete a small trial task modeled on real project work, so both sides see the actual job before committing.
  3. A call with the team follows to walk through your trial task, then a decision shortly after.
