AI evaluation, training data, and applied development.

Northpath Consulting is an AI development and consulting firm in the Atlanta, Georgia area. We produce preference data, write rubrics, design evals, and build tooling for AI labs, data vendors, and technical teams shipping models. Every engagement is fixed in scope: a defined deliverable, a fixed window, and a senior consultant accountable for the result.

Services

  • Model evaluation and red-teaming: rubric design, failure-mode documentation, and adversarial testing before release.
  • Training data and human feedback: preference rankings, rubric-scored ratings, and written critiques for post-training.
  • AI adoption advisory: scoping where a model fits a workflow and what to measure before launch.
  • Custom AI tooling: eval harnesses, annotation interfaces, and dashboards shipped as plain code.

Process

  • 01Scoping call: define the problem, the domain, and what done looks like.
  • 02Fixed-scope proposal: deliverables, timeline, and price in writing.
  • 03Execution: hands-on work with a written update each week.
  • 04Handoff: guidelines, rubrics, code, and notes your team can run with.
